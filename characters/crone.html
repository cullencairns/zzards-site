The Crone

“Have some tea. It’s orobably not a trap.”

Base Cards
Function One
Cure
Cures Poison
Function Two
Fortify
Counters Attack and preserves scoring

Strategy Notes

  • Especially useful when paired with The Maiden.
  • Discard extra Fortify cards cautiously.
Background

Who is The Crone?

The Crone is the window into the old ways. The center of calm that makes everything else feel loud. She is old in the way a forest is old: patient, watchful, and not especially impressed by you.

Likes

  • Silence that knows its place
  • Hot tea
  • Her Cat

Dislikes

  • Brunch
  • The Theatre
  • Helpful suggestions and constructive criticism

Allies

  • The Mother → She has a ways to go, but she's on the right track.
  • The Witch's Cat → He's a good boy.
  • The Maiden → She's eager and brews a decent cuppa.

Rivals

  • The Wizard's Box → She's doesn't like it, she doesn't trust it, and she's certain she's going to break it.
  • Barbarian → Loud, sweaty, and always touching things.

Origin Story

As the tales go, all Crones were once Mothers(or at least Maidens with a decent knowledge of preventative herbs). Deep in the forests, they're the last resort for the needy, the desperate, or the foolish. They brew tea, concoct potions, and do their best to stay out of politics.

The truth is that she has been around long enough that no one remembers when she wasn't. It's not a question of when she came to the forest as much as it is a question of when the forest grew around her.

Now she wanders into games the way fog wanders into valleys: quietly, inevitably, and with a faint sense that you aren't fully in control.

Win & Unique

Win Condition

Score if Attack in The Crone’s Stack is higher than Defense in her Hand.

She's Seen Harder Days, and the more you pile on her the stronger she gets.

Unique Card

Cup of Tea → Play to a chosen Character’s Hand.

  • Deflects 50% of the Attack value on that Character to The Crone.
  • Best used on a beseiged ally, but especially useful on other Crones.
