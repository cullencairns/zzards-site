Origin Story

The Reaper is the personification of that which follows from life. He is the debt collector, not the auditor. He does not come with judgment, but with a bill.

He stands taller than any man I've ever known, his cloaked figure stoic against an ever-present wind. His eye sockets glow with a steady blue spark, his polished skull glistens against the shining light of eternity.

One man has defied him, a Wizard of no real power who just does not seem capable of dying at the appropriate time.

At this point the Reaper is starting to think that Fate wants him to take things into his own hands. It's not good for anyone to be alive for that long.

Win & Unique

Win Condition

Score if you did not discard any cards during your turn, and The Wizard does not have his Hat.

  • Discard no cards during your turn.
  • The Wizard does not have his Hat.

Que Sera, Sera → don’t “fix” your hand with discards. Play the hand you’re dealt unless you’re willing to defy fate.

Unique Card

Memento Mori → Play onto a Character.

  • That Character cannot play any cards for one round.
  • If that Character has Dead Rat, this has no effect.
