Background

Who is The Sausage Salesman?

A cheery merchant with a cart full of suspicious deals. His sausages are legendary, although not for their quality.

Likes

  • Jazz
  • His guests, of course

Dislikes

  • Credit Unions
  • Health Inspectors
  • Competition

Allies

  • The Wizard → Always good for a sausage after a night out.

Rivals

  • The Reaper → Look mate, he makes the clients uncomfortable, is what it is.
  • The Librarian → He won't hear a good business proposition, Sausages and books would be a killer combination.

Origin Story

The Salesman doesn't need much but a ware to dispense and the sincerely held belief that everyone needs what he's selling. Sausages make for the worst salesmen, their ingredients are questionable, the general hygiene of the stall is dubious at best, and the customers desperate enough to buy such a product are often woe to do anything but finish it as quickly as humanly possible.

So he's found a dead pig, fresh enough that a local butcher was willing to grind it for a couple of marks, and now he's set up shop wherever he can get away with it.

His sausages are starting to move, he's found some regulars, and he's starting to wonder whether to expand his territory and open another stall.

Win & Unique

Win Condition

Always. Be. Selling. Score if your hand contains none of The Sausage Salesman's Base Function Cards.

Face Cards don’t count towards the win condition.

Unique Card

Three for One Special → Discard 1 from your hand to draw 3 cards.

  • You may use more than one per round.
  • Counts toward your round’s discards.

Be wary of overextending your hand. You only get so many discards and so many plays. Don't wind up with extra Sausages in your hand.
