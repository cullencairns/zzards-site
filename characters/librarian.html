Background

Who is The Librarian?

The Librarian is the final safeguard of knowledge in a chaotic world.

Likes

  • Silence
  • Well-ordered shelves
  • Soft fruits

Dislikes

  • The Catalogue's incessant whingeing
  • Students (Can't stress this one enough)

Allies

Origin Story

Some say he was once a Wizard, although no one has ever had the courage to ask him. He keeps the books in order, keeping the more dangerous ones under lock and key, tracking them down should they ever escape.

He's kept the Master Catalogue fed on tabloid articles, Clancy Pattersham, and Jodi Roberts books all summer, and it's just now starting its hibernation cycle.

He's finally free to explore, to retrieve some overdue books, and to practice his french. C'est génial.

Win & Unique

Win Condition
