Strategy Notes

Background

Who is The Witch’s Cat?

A smug little prince of shadows with a talent for vanishing with the tastiest morsels on the table. Possibly immortal.

Likes

  • Warm laps
  • Shiny trinkets
  • The Ladies

Dislikes

  • Lcked Doors
  • His belly being touched
  • The Wizard.

Allies

  • The Mother → A warm lap and a guaranteed bowl of milk.
  • The Crone → A reliable source of scritches.
  • The Maiden → A reliable source of food and praise.

Rivals

  • The Librarian → Not nice to cold cats looking for warm nooks.
  • The Wizard → The Reaper hates him, and the Cat trusts the Reaper.

Origin Story

Ownership of the Cat is disputed. At one point twelve different households laid separate claims to him, though none could agree on his name, his favourite food, or where he was at any given time.

The most reliable claims come from the Maiden, Mother, and Crone, none of which actually outright claim "ownership" per se, more...companionship. Ultimately for a bowl of milk and a spot by the fire he'll keep any house rat-free.

Currently he's out of food, entertainment, and foxy ladies, so he's taking his show on the road, on the search for new rodentia to dissect.

Win & Unique

Win Condition

Finders, Keepers. End the round with a Wizard’s Hat, Cup of Tea, and/or Nanny’s Mead in the Witch’s Cat’s Hand.

The Cat scores by collecting trophies. Best to keep someone close by that can help him score.

Unique Card

Dead Rat → Play onto a chosen Character.

  • If a Hand containing Dead Rat is Pilfered Dead Rat is always the stolen card.
  • The Witch’s Cat ignores Dead Rat when Pilfering.
