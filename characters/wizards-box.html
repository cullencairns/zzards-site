The Wizard’s Box

“Does it have...teeth? I swear I saw teeth.”

Strategy Notes

  • Wonderful as a magnet for the Cat to Pilfer off of.
Background

Who is The Wizard’s Box?

A travelling chest with opinions, agency, and a need for personal space. Can allegedly do anything, but appears to specialize in playing Solitaire.

Likes

  • Dry cool closets to plug in and recharge.
  • The Wizard.
  • Software updates (we're not really sure what it means by that)

Dislikes

  • Moisture
  • Viruses
  • Messy Food

Allies

  • The Wizard → The only person who knows how to operate the Box properly.
  • The Politician → Very few people can talk about filing systems for as long as these two.

Rivals

  • The Mother → She's honestly doing her best, but she keeps putting out too much poison.
  • The Crone → Keeps trying to "fix" it. Clearly not "fixing" it.

Origin Story

The Box is the Wizard's best and only real claim to the arcane. Made of an unknown, but apparently autonomous kind of wood, it defends the Wizard with the dogged loyalty of a particularly protective badger.

The Box doesn't seem to mind it's servitude, rather cherishing the opportunity to be useful. It occasionally needs to be kicked repeatedly, something the Wizard calls "rebooting".

It isn't fortune, or fame that has driven the Box out of his usual dry closet, but rather a need to protect his companion from the elements.

Win & Unique

Win Condition

Hydrophobic. The Wizard’s Box does not score if a Cup of Tea, Mother's Mead, or a Cure card is played on the Box.

Keep the Box dry. It can support itself outside of that.

Unique Card

Lost in Transit → Play to a chosen Character.

  • If played against a Character with a Face card in their Hand, return one Face card to the Character the Face Card belongs to.
