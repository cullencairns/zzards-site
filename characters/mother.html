The Mother

“Drink this, and sit up straight.”

Base Cards
Function One
Poison
Reduces odds of scoring by 50%
Function Two
Fortify
Counters Attack and preserves scoring

Strategy Notes

  • Fortify with intention, if you spread your load it's easy for a key character to get flattened.
  • Occasionally she is more useful defending than attacking. Allies can do her dirty work for her.
Background

Who is The Mother?

A sweet, innocent caretaker who will poison you, cuddle you, and then shame you into making different choices.

Likes

  • a competent daughter-in-law (rare, but worth admiring)
  • Punched, sweet, alcoholic, thick, boozy, decadent, delectable, intoxicating, divine drinks
  • Controlled chaos

Dislikes

  • People who “aren’t hungry”
  • Other People's Drama
  • Teetotalers and other virgins

Allies

  • The Maiden → She's a good gel who occasionally makes mistakes, but haven't we all?
  • The Crone → Mutual respect between professionals.
  • The Witch's Cat → He's a cuddly-wuddly goody-woody prince of all he surveys and we love him.

Rivals

  • Barbarian → The stench is unbelievable. Has the man no shame?
  • Wizard's Box → I didn't touch it, I swear. It just started smoking and sparking on its own.

Origin Story

All Mothers are born as Maidens, typically from other Mothers. They learn their craft early, and hone it on unsuspecting, over-willing young gentlemen in dappled glades and daydreams. Eventually they get bored of this and decide to raise children. This Mother was no different.

Her potions usually remedy any unwanted behaviors, and when they don't...she always has her slipper. All other problems go into the ovens, or at least that's what's said to have happened to the last bad batch of her brood.

For now, Mama's going out looking for something new. Something exciting. Something...dangerous.

Win & Unique

Win Condition

Score if at least two enemies are Poisoned.

Mother likes giving out her Bad Apples. Make sure two enemies take their medicine by the end of the round to make Mamma proud.

Unique Card

Mother's Mead → Play to a chosen Character’s Hand.

  • That Character may play no more than 1 card during their next turn.
