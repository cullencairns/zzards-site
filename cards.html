Base Functions

Attack

“Maim, Mutilate, Mangle.”

Prevents scoring on the targeted Character.

“Hold the line.”

Counters Attack and preserves scoring.

“Tastes like almonds.”

“Tastes like a cherry's mid-life crisis.”

Removes Poison.

“Mine now.”
