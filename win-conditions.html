Win Conditions

“Everyone wins differently.”

Quick Reference

Each Character scores independently. Multiple Characters can and should score in the same round.

How To Play a Round

The Wizard

“Hat in Hand.”

End the round with The Wizard's unique card The Wizard's Hat on the Wizard.

The Witch's Cat

“Finders, Keepers”

End the round with one of the unique cards The Wizard's Hat, Cup of Tea, and/or Mother's Mead on the Witch's Cat.

The Politician

“Propaganda.”

Score if two allies end the round holding Distract Cards.

Kong Fuzi

“Pristine Meditation.”

Play no Kong Fuzi cards during your turn.

The Crone

“She's Seen Harder Days.”

Score if The Crone has less Fortify than Attack played against her.

The Mother

“Bad Apples.”

Score if at least two enemies end the round with Poison.

The Wizard's Box

“Hydrophobic.”

Does not score if a Cup of Tea, Mother's Mead, or Cure is played on The Box the round.

The Barbarian

“Pointless Overkill.”

Play one of the Barbarian's Attack Cards on a Poisoned enemy during this round.

The Maiden

“Vanity, Thy Name is Perfect.”

End the round with the Maiden having played the greatest amount of Attack on the board (ties allowed).

The Reaper

“Que Sera, Sera.”

Score if the Player discards no cards during their turn, and The Wizard does not have his Hat in Hand.

The Sausage Salesman

Gotta Sell 'Em All.”

End the round with none of the Salesman's base function cards in the Player's hand.

The Librarian

“Plagiarism.”

Fulfill the Win Condition of a Character that has The Book That Reads You played on them..
