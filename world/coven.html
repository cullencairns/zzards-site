The Coven of Witches

“Whose turn is it to make the tea?”

The Coven

What is the Coven?

Deep in the dark woods, in a conveniently inconvenient part of the Dragon’s Spine is an unnaturally orderly meadow.

There isn't anything worth noting about the meadow. It is adjacent to a very typical woodland cottage (nothing to see there).

The women who frequent the meadow and cottage are minding their own business, and so should you.
