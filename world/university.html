The Illusory Institute

“Do you have a reservation?”

The Institution

What is The University?

The University is where brilliant minds go to become mildly competent, and mildly competent minds go to become permanently employed. There is also quite a well reviewed restaurant on the rooftop.

People come seeking knowledge. They usually leave with diabetes and an itemized bill.

Known For

  • Bottomless mimosas.
  • Eggs Benedict.
  • Shrimp cocktail

Rumors

  • The summer menu is going to have a new Cobb salad
  • The Librarian came up with the Bananas Foster recipe.
  • There's a secret speakeasy under the East Wing called "Faculty Only".
  • One of the line chefs is married to a Genuine Homemaker of Atlantis.
