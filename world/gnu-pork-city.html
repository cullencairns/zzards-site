The City

What is Gnu Pork City?

There are many names for Gnu Pork City, but none come close. The Stinky Onion doesn't quite capture the sheer depth and breadth of GPC's uniquely dense aroma. The Bad Apple doesn't quite convey the level of corruption the politicians blatantly parade. The City of Broken Lights doesn't really do justice to the neglect the impoverished portions of the city endures. No, Gnu Pork City can only be defined by experience.
