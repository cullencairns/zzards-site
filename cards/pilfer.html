Pilfer

“Finders, keepers.”

Effect
← Back to Cards

What does Pilfer do?

Pilfer steals 1 random card from a chosen Character and adds it to the Pilfering Character’s hand.

Rules Notes

  • The stolen card is chosen at random, but will never be an Attack/Poison card.
  • If the target has no cards, Pilfer has no effect.
  • If the target is holding a Dead Rat, Pilfer steals the Dead Rat.

Design Intent

Pilfer introduces controlled chaos. In the right hands, with the right amount of luck, it can turn one win into two.
