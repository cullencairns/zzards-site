Cure

“This might sting.”

Effect
← Back to Cards

What does Cure do?

Cure removes Poison from a chosen Character. Once cured, that Character returns to normal scoring odds.

Rules Notes

  • Cure can stack.
  • Cure only removes 1 Poison per Cure card.
  • Extremely useful against The Wizard’s Box.

Design Intent

Cure is the safety valve for Poison-heavy rounds. It gives players a way to restore reliability.
