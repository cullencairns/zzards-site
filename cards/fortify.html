Fortify

“Hold the line.”

← Back to Cards

What does Fortify do?

Fortify increases a Character’s defense. If the total Fortify Value on a Character is equal to or greater than the Attack Value targeting them, that Character may still score.

Rules Notes

  • A Fortify card has a value from 1 to 5.
  • Fortify values stack on a Character.
  • Fortify only protects against Attack, not other effects.

Design Intent

Fortify exists to reward planning and protection. It allows players to defend key allies and create safe scoring opportunities, but doing so often requires sacrificing tempo elsewhere.
