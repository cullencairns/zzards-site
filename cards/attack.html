What does Attack do?

When the Attack Value played on a Character is greater than the Fortify Value shoring up their Defense they are prevented from scoring.

Rules Notes

  • An attack card carries a value of 1 to 5 representing attack value.
  • Attack affects scoring only, not card play.
  • Attack can be countered by Fortify.

Design Intent

Attack cards are intended to force characters to work cooperatively. By threatening to prevent scoring, characters are encouraged to support each other rather than focusing solely on their own victory.
