Poison

“You’ll feel it later.”

Effect
← Back to Cards

What does Poison do?

A Poisoned Character has their odds of scoring reduced by 50%. Poison persists until removed.

Rules Notes

  • Multiple Poisons do not stack.
  • Poison affects scoring only, not card play.
  • Poison can be removed by Cure.

Design Intent

Poison introduces uncertainty. It doesn't stop a win outright, but it makes relying on a single Character risky.
