What does Distract do?

Distract is played to a Character. While it remains there, it is 2× more likely to be stolen if someone attempts to Pilfer the Character.

Rules Notes

  • Distract sits on a Character as bait.
  • If a hand containing Distract is Pilfered, Distract is twice as likely to be the stolen card.
  • If multiple Distracts are present, each one is considered bait.
  • Necessary for Characters like The Politician to score.

Design Intent

Distract is insurance. It lets you protect higher-value cards, and can overload a character to protect their Fortify and Cure Cards.
